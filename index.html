Mechanisms of Control

UdK Berlin / Winter Semester 2025/26

What is it about?

An experience- and skill-building class focused on learning to design custom mechanisms and software for art projects. Inspired by technologies that manipulate and control objects around us, participants will create their own instruments and machines. Through hands-on lessons involving physical computing (Arduino) and coding, we’ll develop prototypes that encourage reflection on what control means. Together, we’ll explore blending machine precision with creativity, enabling you to craft artful devices.

Below you’ll find the slide decks, demo codes, and references of our weekly sessions. All content is for study use only.

Week 02 — From Electrons to Expression

October 29, 2025

Core Concepts

We talked about how electrons move, what voltage and current mean physically, why resistance matters, and how microcontrollers “see” analog vs. digital signals. We also looked at how this translates into artistic control (light, motion, potentially sound).
