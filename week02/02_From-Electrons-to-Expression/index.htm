Analog Signals

Analog Signals

Characteristics:

  • Represent a continuous range of values (e.g. 0V to 5V)
  • Can capture fine detail — like sound, light, or touch pressure
  • Used by sensors like potentiometers, LDRs, and microphones

Analog Signals

Arduino Use:

  • Read:

    analogRead() — returns 0 to 1023 (10-bit ADC)

  • Write:

    analogWrite() — actually uses PWM, not true analog

Digital Signals

Digital Signals

Characteristics:

  • Only two states: ON (HIGH, 5V) or OFF (LOW, 0V)
  • Clear, fast, and efficient — great for buttons, switches, logic

Digital Signals

Arduino Use:

  • Read: digitalRead() checks if HIGH or LOW
  • Write: digitalWrite() sets pin to HIGH or LOW
